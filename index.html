FAQs

  • When should I file my ITR?
    Typically between April 1st and July 31st of the assessment year. Extensions may apply.
  • What documents are needed for GST filing?
    Invoices, purchase records, bank statements, and GSTIN credentials.
  • Can freelancers and gig workers file ITR?
    Absolutely! We help you file under the correct income head with eligible deductions.
  • How can I lower my taxes legally?
    Through deductions under Sections 80C to 80U, proper asset planning, and rebates. We offer full guidance.
